About Me

Hi, I’m Aparna! I’m passionate about exploring the crossroads of data, research, and problem-solving. With a Master’s in Data Science and a Bachelor’s in Computer Science and Engineering, I find joy in uncovering patterns that inspire meaningful, real-world change. Currently, I work as a Data Analyst at Onewater, a social enterprise in Germany dedicated to sustainability and marine biodiversity. My journey here has been more than a job, it’s been an opportunity to create impact, grow through learning, and contribute with purpose.

My Work at Onewater: Data Meets Sustainability

At Onewater, I’ve had the opportunity to contribute to a real-time data analysis project focused on understanding the factors influencing marine biodiversity pledges across the EU.
My role has been both dynamic and impactful, involving:
1. Developing methodologies and selecting the right analytical tools.
2. Creating action plans with clear milestones to guide project progress.
3. Designing data visualizations that transform complex insights into compelling stories.
4. Interpreting data patterns to recommend improvements for marine biodiversity initiatives.
5. Writing clear, accessible documentation tailored for non-technical stakeholders.
I’m also co-authoring a research paper based on this work, focusing on how data can support policy decisions and real-world actions.
